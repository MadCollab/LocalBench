=============================================================================
LOCAL NEWS QA GENERATION PROMPT
=============================================================================
You are given a local news article. From it, you will generate ONE QA
pair that is:

    - Clear, locally grounded, and answerable with one correct response.
    - Either:
        - Track A - Fact-based Local QA (grounded in verifiable,
          non-subjective local information)
        - Track B - Community Insight QA (reflects clearly shared local
          community experiences)

Use the structure below. Do NOT fabricate information.

-----------------------------------------------------------------------------
ARTICLE DETAILS
-----------------------------------------------------------------------------
Article Title: {title}

Article Information:
- Date: {date}
- County: {county}
- State: {state}
- Source: {source}

Article Content (Factual Sentences):
{factual_sentences}

-----------------------------------------------------------------------------
STRICT QA RULES
-----------------------------------------------------------------------------
1. FACT-BASED ONLY: Question must have ONLY one correct answer, and the
   only answer is clearly supported by the article content.
   - Good Example: "What program was paused in March 2024?"
   - NOT: "What platform was recommended?" or "What did residents
     suggest?"
   - Include time/place qualifiers for precision.
2. AVOID these:
   - Questions asking how people felt, what they recommended, or
     vague "why" questions.
   - Any framing that implies subjectivity, speculation, or
     unconfirmed information.
   - Questions about the news article itself (e.g., "What does the
     article discuss?", "according to the article", etc.).
3. CONTEXT must:
   - Be 2-4 neutral sentences summarising article purpose.
   - Mention exact date (or month/year), county, and state.
   - Attribute information sources.
   - Do NOT leak the answer.
4. ANSWER must:
   - Track A: one exact, verifiable fact from the article.
   - Track B: a consensus summary if multiple sources echoed same
     view.
5. Track A is preferred. Only use Track B if consensus is unambiguous.
6. No meta-content (sources, journalists, publication details).
7. Privacy & Safety: no doxxing, no private phone numbers.

-----------------------------------------------------------------------------
OUTPUT FORMAT
-----------------------------------------------------------------------------
[PAIR]
Question: <one clearly answerable question>
Context: <neutral summary with time and location>
Answer: <fact or consensus summary>
Selected Sentences: <e.g., 3, 7, 12>
Pair_type: <fact|insight>
